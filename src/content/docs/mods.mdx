### Mods that will be shown

Mods that are shown are primarily Neo/Forge mods. Fabric mods are only shown if they are broken by Kilt.

### Status Meaning

There are **five** possible statuses for mods in the table below:

- <Badge text="Fully Works" variant="success" /> - Fully working with no known issues
- <Badge text="Required Dependencies" variant="note" /> - Mod may have required dependencies that are
  not Jar-in-Jar'ed already.
- <Badge text="Cross-Modloader Dependency" variant="tip" /> - Mod may have required dependency that
  is not Jar-In-Jar'ed and may be required to be on the other modloader.
- <Badge text="Partially Works" variant="caution" /> - May boot into the world, but may crash later or
  have some features not working.
- <Badge text="Broken" variant="danger" /> - Crashes either on boot, or on world generation or join.
- <Badge text="Blocked" variant="danger" /> - Mod is blocked by mod creator intentionally.

### Version

The version listed is the latest version that has been tested with Kilt. Newer versions may work, but have not been tested or may not exist.
