### Mods that will be shown

Mods that are shown are primarily Neo/Forge mods. Library mods will only be shown if broken alone. Fabric mods are only shown if they are broken by Kilt. Mods that have very long names may be shortened.

### Status Meaning

There are **six** possible statuses for mods in the table below:

- <Badge text="Fully Works" size="medium" variant="success" /> - Fully working with no known issues
- <Badge text="Required Dependency" size="medium" variant="note" /> - Mod may have required dependencies that are
  not Jar-in-Jar'ed already.
- <Badge text="Cross-Modloader Dependency" size="medium" variant="tip" /> - Mod may have required dependency that
  is not Jar-In-Jar'ed and may be required to be on the opposite modloader.
- <Badge text="Partially Works" size="medium" variant="caution" /> - May boot into the world, but may crash later or
  have some features not working.
- <Badge text="Broken" size="medium" variant="danger" /> - Crashes either on boot, or on world generation or join.
- <Badge text="Blocked" size="medium" variant="danger" /> - Mod is blocked by mod creator intentionally.

### Version

The version listed are the versions that has been tested with Kilt. Other versions may work, but have not been tested or may not exist.

### Notes

Some mods may have additional notes listed in the table, this may be certain issues or additional dependencies that are required.
