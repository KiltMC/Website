### Mods that will be shown

- Mods that are shown are primarily Neo/Forge mods. Library mods will only be shown if broken alone.
- Fabric mods are only shown if they are broken by Kilt.
- Mods that have very long names may be shortened.
- Mods that have Fabric native versions that work will not be shown.

### Status Meaning

There are **five** possible statuses for mods in the table below:

- <Badge text="Fully Works" size="medium" variant="success" /> - Fully working with no known issues, if you find any, make a bug report.
- <Badge text="Cross-Modloader Lib" size="medium" variant="note" /> - Mod may have one or more libraries that is not Jar-In-Jar'ed and may be required to be on the opposite modloader.
- <Badge text="Partially Works" size="medium" variant="caution" /> - Has features that may not work as intended.
- <Badge text="Broken" size="medium" variant="danger" /> - Crashes at any point.
- <Badge text="Blocked" size="medium" variant="danger" /> - Mod is blocked by mod creator intentionally.

### Version

The version listed are the versions that has been tested with Kilt. Other versions may work, but have not been tested or may not exist.

### Notes

Some mods may have additional notes listed in the table, this may be certain issues, or information like requiring additional libraries.


### Mod List
Mods are not alphabetically sorted nor status sorted, this may change in the future. To see if your mod of choice works, use your browser's search feature.
