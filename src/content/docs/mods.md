### Mods that will be shown

Mods that are shown are primarily Neo/Forge mods. Fabric mods are only shown if they are broken by Kilt.

### Status Meaning

There are **five** possible statuses for mods in the table below:

- <span style="color: rgb(6, 214, 160)">**Fully Works**</span> - Fully working with no known issues.
- <span style="color: rgb(17, 138, 178)">**Required Dependencies**</span> - Mod may have required dependencies that are
  not Jar-in-Jar'ed already.
- <span style="color: rgb(106, 76, 147)">**Cross-Modloader Dependency**</span> - Mod may have required dependency that
  is not Jar-In-Jar'ed and may be required to be on the other modloader.
- <span style="color: rgb(247, 140, 107)">**Partially Works**</span> - May boot into the world, but may crash later or
  have some features not working.
- <span style="color: rgb(239, 71, 111)">**Broken**</span> - Crashes either on boot, or on world generation or join.
- <span style="color: gray">**Blocked**</span> - Mod is blocked by mod creator intentionally.

### Version

The version listed is the latest version that has been tested with Kilt. Newer versions may work, but have not been tested or may not exist.
